Welcome to the Van Gogh Archives Souvenir Page


Here you can purchase three professionally designed replicas of Van Goghs best work including:

Portrait of Dr. Gachet

One of the most revered paintings by Van Gogh. It depicts Dr. Paul Gachet, a homeopathic doctor and artist with whom van Gogh resided following a spell in an asylum at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence. Gachet took care of Van Gogh during the final months of his life

Starry Night Sky

Starry Night is an oil on canvas painted in June 1889, it depicts the view from the east-facing window of Van Gogh's asylum room at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an imaginary village.

Sunflowers

Sunflowers is one of the most popular and well known paintings by Van Gogh. It was also the picture that Van Gogh was most proud of. It was painted during a rare period of excited optimism, while Van Gogh awaited the arrival of his hero, the avant-garde painter Paul Gauguin.
